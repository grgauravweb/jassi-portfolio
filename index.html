 Kala Construction lead by Jitesh Sable is a firm in consultancy, design and execution of architectural, exterior  and engineering projects with some of the most prestigious clients in Public, Corporate and Private Sectors.The Firm was established  to fulfil a long felt need for architectural and engineering knowhow in governmental, public and private building, educational campuses. Industrial townships, and indeed, in all facets of the construction industry. From a modest beginning, Kala Construction has now grown up to be the leading firm of Architects, Interior Designers and Consulting Engineers .

A whole and adequate exposure in design and planning of Interiors through our in-house support members and specialized outsourced teams elevates "Designers’ Guild" into a full-fledged industry in Interior design and consultancy projects on a National Level.

We at Kala Construction, with our profound experience have emerged as one of the leading firms  to practice Green Building and Sustainable Architecture including various educational programs and technical upgradations by our Consultants and other vendors.

WHAT WE DO

We offer solutions in comprehensive Design ,consultancy, and PMC of projects for:

Residential, Industrial and Commercial Complexes (Architecture)

Exterior  Design

Educational Campuses (Architecture)

Sports Facilities (Architecture)

Machine and Equipment Foundations (Civil)

Pile Foundations (Civil)

Power Distribution Systems (Electrical)

Steel Structures (Civil)

Our expertise also extends to utility services like Green Building Simulation, MEP including HVAC, Interiors and Landscaping.
